Seeing the structure and taxonomy replaces rote memorization.

## The Aerial View


Memorizing isolated pieces prevents true comprehension.


Seeing the whole picture provides context for future decisions.


Taxonomy organizes chaos into manageable systems.


## The Bicycle Moment


True understanding arrives in a single moment of clarity.


**Before the snap, you fail**; after the snap, you ride.


Refining technique follows the initial structural realization.


## Rethinking the Curriculum


### Calculus vs. Statistics


Schools force calculus out of sheer habit.


Statistics provides modern, practical utility for daily life.


**Curriculum design** **must prioritize concepts people easily see and apply.**


## Distilling Core Principles


### The Evolution Example


Teaching Mendelian genetics overwhelms beginners.


Three simple rules explain the entire system: organisms reproduce, offspring possess variations, the environment selects survivors.


**Mastering core forks in the road enables self-directed exploration.**
